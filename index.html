About The Experience

We organise curated travel experiences to Georgia for small groups of travelers looking for authentic destinations before mass tourism arrives.

Our trips combine culture, nature, food and history in one unforgettable journey across the Caucasus.

  • Small groups (8-15 people)
  • Authentic local experiences
  • Carefully designed itineraries
  • European travelers community

7 Day Itinerary

Day 1 – Kutaisi

Arrival, airport pickup and visit to the Green Bazaar.

Day 2 – Martvili Canyon & Prometheus Cave

Boat ride through turquoise canyon and exploration of one of Georgia’s most famous caves.

Day 3 – Road to Tbilisi

Visit Gori, the ancient cave city of Uplistsikhe and UNESCO town of Mtskheta.

Day 4 – Explore Tbilisi

Old Town, Narikala Fortress, sulphur baths and panoramic viewpoints.

Day 5 – Kazbegi & Military Highway

Drive through the dramatic Caucasus mountains to Gergeti Trinity Church.

Day 6 – Free Day

Explore Tbilisi at your own pace, wine tasting or markets.

Day 7 – Return

Transfer back to Kutaisi and departure.
